FAQ

Common questions

What happens after I click Submit?

Your default email app (Mail, Gmail, Outlook, etc.) will open with all of your information pre-filled and addressed to the developer. Just review it, attach any screenshots if you have them, and tap Send.

How long until I hear back?

Music Folder is maintained by a single developer. Most reports receive a first response within a few days. Critical bugs are prioritized.

Is my information kept private?

Yes. The email is sent only to the developer. Your email address is used solely to follow up about your issue.

The email app didn't open. What now?

Email musicfolder.app@gmail.com directly with your details. This can happen if no default email app is set up on your device.
